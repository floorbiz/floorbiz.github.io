PRESSURE WASHING, DRIVEWAY & PATIO CLEANING SERVICES FOR LEICESTERSHIRE

CleanPave are a professional pressure washing and hard surface restoration company based in Syston, Leicestershire who are proud to be a member of the Mister Group. We have invested considerable resources in acquiring the expertise and professional quality pressure washing equipment to ensure our customers receive a top quality service. We work extensively throughout the county covering Sileby, Rothley, Coalville, Quorn, Leicester, Loughborough, Melton Mowbray, Glenfield, Groby, Shepshed, Barrow on Soar, Thurmaston, Birstall and Markfield plus many more.

CleanPave are able to clean the majority of hard surface finishes bringing them back to that as "good as new" look.

We can also re-colour pattern imprinted concrete, restore tired tarmac and oil wooden decking all after a through pre-clean.

As registered installers of Smartseal's proven, top quality sealants available in a variety of finishes we can give your block paving through to Indian stone patios a protective finish that will aid in inhibiting weed growth and visually make a dramatic enhancement to your home. The sealants will also protect the surface from UV light and staining from oil, grease and tyres.

Our pricing is very competitive but we believe the standard of work we achieve is second to none and we are very confident you will be completely satisfied with the way we work whilst at your home.
