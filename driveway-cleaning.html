Driveway Cleaning

CleanPave provide a professional driveway cleaning and sealing service for Leicestershire and Rutland.

Driveway cleaning is at the heart of our business. Many thousands of homeowners across Leicestershire have paid a lot of money for driveways in various surfaces to be laid, but then been disappointed when the surface deteriorates due to dirt, grime, moss and algae formation.

If required a pre-treatment of a weed killer and/or a fungicidal treatment will be applied prior to undertaking a through pressure wash of the area.

Working with our professional level equipment generating cleaning pressure in excess of 200 bar,+3000 psi, we are able to remove the toughest of dirt ,algae staining that has built up over a period of time. Whether your driveway is block or crazy paved we can carry out a regular maintenance programme preferably on a yearly basis to ensure your investment is clean tidy and looking well kept. If your drive is block paved then after a suitable drying time kiln dried sand is swept back between the pavers to give a wonderful clean finish. If you require a more permanent and longer lasting finish then we are able to apply Smartseals top quality sealants. 

As registered installers for Smartseal paving sealers in Leicestershire we can also supply their products for a DIY job which will help to keep your paving protected in the long term.
