Decking & Wood Cleaning

If  block paving is the most popular of paving materials in use nowadays then wooden decking is a close second as an additional enhancement to thousands of homes up and down the country. Popularised by endless home make over and gardening programmes all have no hesitation in making clear the benefits of an installation to your home or business.

What these shows don't tell you is that within a very short space of time that expensive decking can very quickly loose it's initial visual appeal. With the climatic conditions we experience providing the perfect breeding ground for significant growths of moss and algae that can over time and in exceptional circumstances make that deck a very dangerous place to venture onto. CleanPavecan with the utmost care fully refurbish your decking and wooden furniture . After an application of a fungicidal wash and a good scrub to ensure the spores of the algae and moss are fully killed off we then pressure wash the entire area. The use of a lance is limited to avoid any striping or banding that can occur through over concentrated pressure applied to the surface we use our rotary headed cleaner that distributes the pressure more evenly to avoid any banding problems.

We can if required then apply top quality oils to further protect and enhance the decked areas. To keep your deck in tip top condition an appropriate application of oil is recommend on a six monthly basis.

CleanPave specialise in cleaning decking in Leicestershire and Loughborough.
