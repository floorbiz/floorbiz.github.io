Commercial Cleaning

CleanPave also offers a comprehensive range of exterior cleaning services to the commercial market and business enterprises located throughout the county of Leicestershire.

Our investment in pressure cleaning equipment of the highest professional standards will ensure we can quote and be extremely confident of carrying out a cleaning and refurbishment project regardless of size. 

CleanPave works at all times to comply with HSE guidelines and for our  commercial work a full risk assessment is carried out.

If you have a public access area that has started to exhibit signs of wear and tear and perhaps significant moss and algae growth that may render your deck or patio unsafe then CleanPave is able to to fully restore and enable that site to ensure your visitors are met with a safe and aesthetically pleasing environment.
