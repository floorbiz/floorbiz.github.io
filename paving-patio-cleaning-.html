Paving & Patio Cleaning

CleanPave provide a professional patio and paving slab cleaning service for Leicestershire and Rutland.

Most patios are constructed using very porous materials and this tends to lead to moisture retention, which in turn makes patio slabs very prone to infestation by weeds, algae and moss. If the area isn't jet-washed regularly it will look neglected and uninviting and make maintenance that much more difficult.

CleanPave have invested a great deal of money in purchasing the very best equipment, including a Sigma Honda Petrol-engined pressure washer, which operates at far higher pressures than the typical unit a homeowner may own. In addition, we use a large rotary-headed cleaner which uses many water jets to speed up the cleaning process.

To keep your patio looking at it's best for the longer term, not just in the first few weeks after cleaning, we will always recommend the application of Smartseal™ Patio Sealer. This high quality product will make the patio slabs less prone to water retention, making a less attractive environment for green algae, moss, weeds and lichen to grow. It will also enhance the natural colours of your paving slabs.
